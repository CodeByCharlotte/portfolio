About Me

I'm Charlotte—a student working toward a certification in Front End Development via The Learning People's Front End PLUS course. My journey began with a degree in Music from Bangor University back in 2009. From there, I developed a career in Customer Service and Sales, all while maintaining the responsibilities of being a carer within my family.


When a twist of fate led to the need for a career reboot, I drew inspiration from my general exploration of coding over the years—from toying with BASIC on my VTEC laptop and experiments with HTML in the early days of the internet to more recent endeavors with Twitch alerts. This, alongside valuable insights from industry professionals, proved a pivotal compass in pointing me towards the world of Front End Development.


Outside of my studies, I'm a gamer and a fan of the Final Fantasy series, with FFX topping my list. But if you can't find me, I've probably wandered off to Eorzea. Maybe I'm questing, amassing a gil fortune, or simply checking out the latest fashions on those idling in Limsa.


Upon achieving my certification in Front End Development, my journey doesn't end—it's just beginning. I plan to delve deeper into my studies, fueled by curiosity, and to stay abreast of the ever-evolving landscape. Or, to put it another way, once I'm done with this quest, it's time to move onto the next one!
